Greener Mailbox

The aim of this tool is to help you clean the e-mails contained in your box mail in order

to reduce the CO2 emission coming from mails storage.

Here is the operating mode:

1. Please choose an user to show his mailbox

2. Then, you can click on Good practices, in the Greenbox button drop-down,

to clean the mail box from mails containing no or little information

3. To beneficiate of our removal recommendation system, you can click on Removal recommendation,

in the Greenbox button drop-down. It will propose you a number of mails that are selected thanks to our

algorithm with a high probability of deletion. You can choose the ones you want to delete

and then press on the Clean up button to suppress them.

4. Then check the benefice of our actions on the button Statistics, in the Greenbox button drop-down.
